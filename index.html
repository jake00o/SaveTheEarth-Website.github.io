Welcome to Save the Earth NGO

Our mission is to protect and preserve the environment for future generations. Join us in making a difference!

Who We Are

Save the Earth NGO is dedicated to raising awareness about environmental issues and promoting sustainable practices. We work on various projects including tree planting, beach cleanups, and environmental education programs.

Our Activities

  • Tree Planting: Join us in planting trees to combat deforestation and climate change.
  • Beach Cleanups: Help us clean and protect our oceans and marine life.
  • Environmental Education: Participate in workshops and seminars to learn more about sustainable living.
